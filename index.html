Who We Are
關於美味閣

A Home for Noodle Lovers

米線愛好者的家

Noodle Fusion 美味閣 — meaning "House of Delicious Flavour" — brings the soul of Asian noodle culture to Markham. We slow-cook our broths, hand-select our ingredients, and craft every bowl as if it were for family. Whether it's our signature rice noodle (米線) in a clear, savoury broth or a richer fusion creation, each dish is a labour of love made fresh to order.

美味閣，顧名思義是「美食薈萃之閣」，將亞洲麵食文化的精髓帶到萬錦市。我們慢火熬製湯底，嚴選食材，每一碗都如同為家人精心烹調。無論是清澈鮮美的招牌米線，還是風味濃郁的創意融合料理，每道菜都是現點現做的心意之作。

Find us inside the food court at 9390 Woodbine Avenue, where a warm bowl is always waiting.

我們位於 9390 Woodbine Avenue 美食廣場內，一碗熱騰騰的米線隨時等候您的到來。

Two Topping Rice Noodle
